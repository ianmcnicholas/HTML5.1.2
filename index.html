My favourite foods

As of April 2023... subject to change without notice

A traditional dish from distant lands, typically southern Asia, the Curry has become a staple dish in the UK.

With a curry house around every corner, and an almost infinite variety of dishes, curry can please even the pickiest of diners.
Whether enjoying a romantic evening with a loved one, or heading out on the town with your friends, a trip to a curry house has become a go-to event for many nights out in the UK.

Ian's Yum Factor: 70%


Once confined to the solitudes of a builder's cafe, the Full English is enjoyed by all.

Whether from a greasy 'spoons, or from a gourmet vegan joint, the full english has evolved and adapted to meet all dietary requirements.
Eggs any way you want them, hash browns or not, bacon or facon if you desire, anyone can enjoy this classical English dish.

Ian's Yum Factor: 80%


Heading to the Spanish coast, a Paella will leave you feeling full and satisfied.

This one-pot traditional Spanish dish can be made anyway you want it, and goes down particularly well with a cervaca or two.
A great dish to impressive with at the dining table, a paella will feed the masses, and not leave you out of pocket.

Ian's Yum Factor: 70%


A world-wide favourite, the Italian Pizza is the ultimate comfort food.

Supermarkets will sell them to you frozen for pennies, or you can make your own dough for 24 hours beforehand - pizza will comfort both the slob in you and the chef in you.
Whether you want an easy "lob it in the oven" dinner, or want to have a fun date-night adding your own toppings to home-made dough, a pizza will put a smile on your face anytime.

Ian's Yum Factor: 100%


An easy, warming dish for a cold winter's day, a simple soup will sooth your cockles like nothing else.

As simple as chop, boil, blend, a soup is great way to get rid of leftover veggies, or experiment with new ones. A world-dish means you can make it however you like.
I remember the home-made soup my mother would make us on a cold day as a child, and now I can make it myself, with just 3 ingredients. Simply delicious!

Ian's Yum Factor: 70%
